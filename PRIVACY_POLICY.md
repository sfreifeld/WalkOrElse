# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 28, 2026_

## 1) Overview
WalkOrElse is a personal MVP web app that helps track daily step counts from Oura and can optionally post to X (Twitter) if a step goal is not met.

This Privacy Policy explains what data is used, why it is used, and your choices.

## 2) Data We Collect
WalkOrElse only collects and stores the minimum data needed to run the app:

- **Step data**: daily step count fetched from Oura.
- **Operational timestamps**: such as `last_checked_at` to track when data was last refreshed.
- **User settings**: such as timezone and cutoff time.
- **API credentials/tokens you provide**: Oura API token and (if enabled) X API credentials/tokens for posting.

### What we do **not** intentionally collect
- No payment or billing information.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No sale of personal data.
- No broad profile data beyond what is listed above.

## 3) How We Use Data
We use data only to operate the app features you enable, including:

- Displaying daily step progress.
- Checking whether your daily goal was met.
- Running automations based on your settings.
- Posting to X on your behalf **only if you enable/configure that behavior**.
- Basic troubleshooting, maintenance, and reliability of the app.

## 4) Third-Party Services
WalkOrElse relies on third-party services to function:

- **Oura API**: to fetch step/activity data.
- **X (Twitter) API**: to publish posts if you configure posting.
- **Vercel (hosting/infrastructure)**: to host and run the web app.

Your use of these integrations is also subject to each provider's own terms and privacy policies.

## 5) Data Sharing
- WalkOrElse does **not** sell your data.
- WalkOrElse does **not** share your data with unrelated third parties for marketing.
- Data is only sent to third parties as needed to deliver core functionality (for example, Oura fetches and optional X posting).

## 6) Storage and Retention
WalkOrElse uses simple file-based or lightweight database storage.

Data is retained only as long as reasonably needed to operate the app, unless you request deletion sooner. Because this is an MVP, backups/logs may temporarily retain limited records for reliability and debugging.

## 7) Your Rights and Choices
You can request to:

- **Access** the data stored about you.
- **Correct/update** settings data.
- **Delete** your stored data and tokens.
- **Disable automation** (including X posting) by removing credentials or updating configuration.

To make a request, use the contact method below.

## 8) Security
Reasonable efforts are used to protect stored data and tokens. However, no method of storage, transmission, or internet service is 100% secure. You use the service with that understanding.

## 9) Children's Privacy
WalkOrElse is not intended for children under 13.
